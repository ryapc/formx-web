## Why We Built Smart Learning

Most organizations face two pain points when using AI for document processing:

  1. **Static AI models don’t improve:** If the extractor makes the same mistake repeatedly such as adding a prefix to invoice numbers, users have to manually fix outputs every time.
  2. **Too much reliance on IT teams:** Adjusting AI models often requires technical expertise. Business teams are left waiting for fixes, which delays projects and drains IT resources.



Smart Learning solves both challenges by putting control back in the hands of end users. You can teach your extractor the right rules as you work - no technical background required. Each correction is saved as new knowledge, making the system more accurate, robust, and aligned with your needs.

‍

## How Smart Learning Works

Getting started is simple. Here’s how you can train and validate your extractor in just a few steps:

‍

### Step 1. Upload & Extract

Upload your documents such as invoices, receipts, or shipping records, and FormX will automatically capture the key fields based on your schema. No setup required.

‍

### Step 2. Refine with Smart Learning

Use Smart Learning to fine-tune results. Upload a sample file, adjust the fields (for example, remove unwanted prefixes in invoice numbers), and save.

FormX remembers your corrections and applies them to future documents, so you never have to fix the same error twice.

‍

### Step 3. Test & Validate

Run the extractor again on the same document to confirm FormX has learned the new format. Over time, each correction compounds improving performance and accuracy across your workflows.

‍

### Step 4. Organize in Workspaces

Create a workspace, link it to an extractor, and upload multiple files for batch processing. This makes it easy to keep projects organized while training your extractor at scale.

‍

### Step 5. Export Results

Export the extracted data in JSON or Excel format. You can download results as individual files or combine them into a single consolidated report which is ready for integration into your systems.
