## **Introducing FormX.ai ImageChecker: Capture with Confidence!**

Ever submitted a captured document only to realize the image quality was terrible? Was the text blurry, or maybe a crucial part of the document got cut off? We've all been there.

Introducing **FormX.ai ImageChecker** , your new best friend for ensuring flawless document submissions. This innovative feature analyzes the image quality of your captured documents in real-time. **ImageChecker** assesses various aspects and assigns a quality score, so you know exactly if the image is good to go or needs a retake.  
‍

## **See Before You Submit: Avoid Costly Delays**

With **FormX.ai ImageChecker** , you can:

  * **Eliminate blurry images:** **ImageChecker** detects blurry text and imperfections, preventing submissions that might be rejected due to poor quality.
  * **Avoid document cutoff:** No more frustration over missing information! **ImageChecker** ensures the entire document is captured within the frame.
  * **Say goodbye to glare:** **ImageChecker** flags images affected by glare, so you can capture a clear version instead.
  * **Keep text in focus:** Worried about text being cut off or too small to read? **ImageChecker** identifies these issues, allowing you to capture a clear and complete image.
  * **Minimize noise and faint text:** **ImageChecker** detects noisy images and faint text, ensuring optimal clarity for accurate data processing.



**FormX.ai ImageChecker** empowers you to capture documents with confidence, saving you time and frustration. No more wasted submissions due to poor image quality.
