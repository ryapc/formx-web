‍**The Strategic Shift to Enterprise and Data Automation**

Enterprise automation is the systematic replacement of manual, repetitive workflows with digital processes powered by Machine Learning (ML) and Robotic Process Automation (RPA). By integrating data automation, businesses can collect and organize information without human intervention, reducing operational costs by up to 30% while ensuring 100% data integrity for financial reporting.

By combining machine learning and low-code platforms, companies can automate every possible business process, creating a "single source of truth" that allows for audit-ready accuracy across all departments without the "human element" causing rote entry mistakes.

## **Transitioning from Standard OCR to Intelligent Document Processing (IDP)**

While traditional OCR software (Optical Character Recognition) converts images into raw text, Intelligent Document Processing (IDP) utilizes machine learning to understand the context of that text. This evolution allows for the automatic extraction of specific fields, such as "Net Amount" or "Vendor Name", directly into structured formats like JSON or CSV.

**Q: What is the difference between OCR and IDP?**

**A:** OCR (Optical Character Recognition) [converts images into raw text](<../tools/invoice-ocr-api.html>) while IDP (Intelligent Document Processing) also utilises OCR technology but then uses AI to interpret that text, identifying the relationship between data points (e.g., matching a "Total" to a specific "Currency").
