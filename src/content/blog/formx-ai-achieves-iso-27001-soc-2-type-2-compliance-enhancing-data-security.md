## What Are ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type 2?

ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type 2 are two of the most prestigious certifications in the realm of information security and data protection.

  * **ISO 27001** : This international standard defines best practices for implementing and maintaining an information security management system (ISMS). It ensures organizations systematically manage sensitive data, mitigate risks, and continually improve their security measures.**‍**
  * **SOC 2 Type 2** : Developed by the American Institute of CPAs (AICPA), SOC 2 focuses on the operational effectiveness of an organization's systems over time, particularly concerning security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy. The "Type 2" designation means that an independent audit has verified that these controls function effectively over an extended period.



Together, these certifications validate an organization's commitment to rigorous security protocols and its ability to consistently protect customer data. FormX.ai’s achievement of both underscores our dedication to maintaining the highest standards in safeguarding information.
