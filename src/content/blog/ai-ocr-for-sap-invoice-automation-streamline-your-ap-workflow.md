SAP is the backbone of enterprise finance operations, but getting invoice data into SAP still relies heavily on manual data entry. AP teams spend hours keying vendor details, line items, and amounts from paper invoices and PDFs into SAP - a repetitive process that drains resources and introduces costly errors.

AI-powered OCR tools like FormX can automate this workflow. By extracting structured data from invoices via API and exporting it in formats ready for SAP import, FormX eliminates the manual bottleneck between receiving an invoice and processing it in your ERP.

## The Problem: Manual Invoice Processing in SAP

### What the Typical AP Workflow Looks Like

Without automation, AP teams follow this process for every invoice: receive the document (email, mail, or portal), open the invoice, manually type vendor details into SAP, enter line items one by one, verify totals against PO, save and route for approval.

### Why This Is Costly

**Time drain:** Manual invoice processing takes 4-6 minutes per invoice. For a property management firm processing thousands of invoices monthly, this consumes entire teams.

**Error rate:** Manual data entry has a typical error rate of 3-5%. Mistyped amounts lead to reconciliation issues, duplicate payments, and audit findings.

**Scaling bottleneck:** As invoice volume grows across entities or properties, you need more data entry staff - or your AP process falls behind.
