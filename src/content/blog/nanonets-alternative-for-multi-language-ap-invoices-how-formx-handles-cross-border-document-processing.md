## The Core Challenge: Why Multi-Language Invoice Processing is Hard

Processing international invoices isn't just about translating text; it's about understanding context, which is where many automated systems fail. The costs of getting it wrong are significant, with manual processing costing between $15-$25 per hour and carrying a 3-5% error rate.

The primary challenges include:

  * **Language and Formatting Barriers:** Invoices arrive with different languages, date formats (DD-MM-YYYY vs. MM-DD-YYYY), and currency symbols, confusing standard OCR tools.
  * **Regulatory Complexity:** Navigating the patchwork of global tax laws, like VAT and GST, requires a system that can adapt to different rules for each region.
  * **High Cost of Manual Work:** When automation fails, teams revert to manual data entry, which is slow, expensive, and prone to errors. Traditional OCR's failure to handle mixed-language documents forces many companies into maintaining separate, costly workflows for different regions.
